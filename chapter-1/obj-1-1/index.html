Articles made using semantic HTML can also have a header tag, as headers describe the start of any section of a HTML document (not just the whole thing)

The above two headers were grouped into a <hgroup> element. This element is not supported by every browser and it has been dropped from the W3C speicifcation of HTML5. It was intended to affect how grouped headings are displayed.

What are <article> tags used for?

The article tag is meant to signify a self-containing document, such as a new article, blog post, recipe etc.

What are articles made up of?

Articles can contain text and other content, including other semantic HTML elements like <figure>, <figcaption>

Articles can also be separated up into <sections >. In fact, this is the second section of the article in this document
